# Carne Guisada 
## Ingredients
- 1 green bell pepper 
- 1 medium onion 
- 3 cloves garlic 
- 2 large tomatoes 
- 3lb boneless beef shoulder roast (or stew meat)
- 2 tsp ground cumin 
- 1 TBSP lard (or vegetable or olive oil)
- 1 TBSP salt 
- 1 tsp ground pepper 
- 1/2 cup water 
- 1 TBSP all purpose flour 

## Directions 
1.  In a large stovetop pan, add lard or oil and turn heat to medium. 
2.  Cut up roast to 1 inch cubes and add to pan.  Roughly chop the tomato and add on top of meat. 
3.  Remove seeds from bell pepper.  Rough chop and add to the top of ingredients. 
4.  Rough slice onion and add to pan. 
5.  Chop garlic cloves and add to pan. 
6.  Add cumin, salt and pepper. 
7.  Now, stir all ingredients together.  
8.  Place lid on top and cooko on low for 3 hours. 
9.  30 minutes before carne guisada is ready, add flour to 1/2 c water.  Stir into pot. 
10.  Let simmer on low for the next 20-30 minutes.

Serve with traditional Mexican rice and home made flour tortillas.
