# Samosa Chaat
## Ingredients
### For the Chole 
- 2 c dried chickpeas, soaked overnight 
- 5-6 whole green cardamom 
- 4-5 black peppercorn 
- 2 bay leaves 
- 2 1 inch cinnamon stick 
- 2 black tea bags 
- 4-5 cup black water 
- salt to taste
- 1 TBSP oil 
- 3/4 cup pureed onion 
- 2 tsp ginger garlic paste 
- 2.5 cup pureed tomatoes 
- 3 tsp chole masala 
- 1/2 tsp red chili powder 
- 1/2 tsp paprika powder 
- 3/4 tsp cumin powder 

### For Serving
- Samosa - (frozen)
- Plain yogurt or sour cream 
- chutneys 
- copped onion 
- sev (crunchy topping)
- pomegranate arils 
- chaat masala 

## Directions 
1.  Soak dried chick peas overnight in 4 cups water.  In the morning, drain and rinse. 
2.  Transfer to a pressure cooker.  Add the whole spices, black tea bags, water (4-5 c) and salt.  Pressure cook until complete.  (One pot bean setting)  
3.  Heat oil in a pan on medium he at.  Once the oil is hot, add grated onions to it.  Cook the onions until the raw smell goes away completely, around 5-6 minutes.  Add the ginger garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.  
4.  Add the pureed tomatoes and mix.  Cover and cook for 15 minutes on medium heat, stir once or twice.  Once cooked, add the spices.  Add the chick peas into the pan and stir.  Cover and let curry simmer for about 30 minutes on low-medium heat.  
5.  Serve over samosas, with yogurt, chutney, onion, sev, pomegranate and chaat.
