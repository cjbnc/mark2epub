# Ropa Vieja Crock Pot Recipe 
## Ingredients
- 2 lb beef chuck, thinly sliced
- 1 large onion, sliced
- 1 green bell pepper, sliced
- 1 red or yellow bell pepper, sliced 
- 3 roma tomatoes, cut into strips 
- 1 jalapeno (optional) chopped 
- 10 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/2 cup cilantro, chopped 
- 2 cup chicken or beef broth 
- 1/2 cup dry white wine 
- 1 TBSP red wine vinegar
- 1-2 bay leaves 
- 1 16 oz can crushed tomatoes 
- 1 6 oz can tomato paste
- 1 small jar red roasted pepper strips 
- 2 tsp dried oregano 
- 2 tsp cumin
- 1 packet sazon 
- 1 tsp smoked paprika 
- 1 tsp adobo 
- 1/2 cup olives (optional)
- 2 tsp salt 
- 1 lime 
## Directions 
1.  Cut meat into thin strips.  Season with salt and adobo if using.  Brown meat in a heavy skillet with a few tablespoons of oil.  Add all ingredients (except cilantro and olives) in slow cooker on high for 4 hours or 6 hours set to low.  At end of time, add cilantro and olives.   Serve with white rice.  Traditionally also served with stewed black beans.
