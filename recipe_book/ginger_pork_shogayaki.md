# Ginger Pork Shogayaki
## Ingredients
- 1 21 oz pork loin, thinly sliced. 
- 3 medium onions, sliced 
- 3 TBSP vegetable oil 
- salt, to taste 
- 5 TBSP soy sauce 
- 2 TBSP ginger juice
- 2 TBSP sake 
- 2 TBSP sugar 
## Directions 
1.  Grate ginger and press to extract 2T of juice.  Mix soy sauce, ginger juice, sake and sugar in small bowl, and set aside.  Sprinkle pork evenly with salt. 
2.  Heat the oil in a frying pan, over high heat and saute the pork slices on one side until each is a light brown color. Then turn and saute on the other side to the same color. 
3.  Once all the meat is lightly browned, add the onions. 
4.  When the onions become transparent, add the sauce and mix. 
5.  Continue to cook the meat, onions and sauce on medium high heat until the sauce thickens.  Once the sauce thickens and is mixed with the pork and onion, the dish is ready.  

Serve with a side vegetable (broccoli) and rice.
