# Sweet and Spicy Korean Style Potatoes
## Ingredients
- 3 russet potatoes 
- 2 tsp vegetable oil 
- 1 tsp minced garlic 
- 1 tsp chili pepper powder (can omit to reduce heat)
- 2 TBSP Gochujang
- 2 TBSP honey 
- 1 TBSP ketchup
- 2 TBSP soy sauce 
- 2 TBSP cold water 
- 1 TBSP sesame seeds 
## Directions 
1.  Peel 3 potatoes.  Cut them into 1-inch cubes. 
2.  Add potato cubes to a medium sauce pan, cover with 1 inch of cold wate.r  Bring to a boil over high heat. 
3.  Use low heat to simmer for 10 minutes. 
4.  Meanwhile, make sauce by mixing all sauce ingredients in a large bowl. Set aside. 
5.  Turn off the heat, draiin the boiled potatoes, transfer them to a large bowl. 
6.  Add 2 T of vegetable oil to the the potatoes.  Mix well. 
7.  Put the potatoes in a large frying pan.  Use medium high heat to pan fry each side until golden brown.  (it takes about 1 1/2 minute to pan fry each side )
8.  Turn off the heat.  Add the potatoes to the sauce.  Mix well. 
9.  Can be served hot immediately, or keep in the fridge up to two days.  Serve cold as a side dish.
