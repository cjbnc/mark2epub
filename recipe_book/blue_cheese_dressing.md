# Blue Cheese Dressing

Source: [Sip and Feast](https://www.sipandfeast.com/blue-cheese-dressing/)

- 3 oz blue cheese
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 1/2 cup buttermilk, plus extra to thin
- 1 TBSP lemon juice
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mash the blue cheese with a fork.
2. Add mayonnaise, buttermilk, lemon juice, and garlic powder and whisk
   to combine. If the dressing is too thick, add a bit more buttermilk
   at a time until the desired consistency is achieved.
3. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy!

## Notes

- Dressing can be saved in the fridge for up to 7 days.
