# Lao Laab Gai 
## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground meat (beef, chicken, pork, turkey)
- 2 TBSP canola or olive oil 
- 2 shallots or 1 small onion, thinly sliced 
- 1 small can green chile 
- 2 garlic cloves, minced 
- 2 TBSP lime juice
- 3 TBSP fish sauce 
- 1 TBSP okonomi or oyster sauce 
- 1 tsp Sriracha
- 2-3 green onions 
## Directions 
1.  Cook the chicken over medium heat, using a few T of water if needed.  Break it up while it cooks.  It shouldn't be brown.  Drain and set in a bowl. 
2.  Add oil to the pan over medium high heat.  Cook the shallot or onions with the green chile until tender, 3-4 minutes.  Add garlic and cook for one more minute. 
3.  Turn off the heat.  Add the meat back to the pan and stir in the sauces.  Add the green oions and mix in. 
4.  Serve warm with rice, lettuce or cabbage leaves and cucumber slices.
