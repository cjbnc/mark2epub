# Chicken Cassoulet Soup 
## Ingredients
- 1/2 lb bulk pork sausage 
- 5 cup water 
- 1 can (16oz) kidney beans 
- 1 can (15oz) black beans 
- 1 can (15oz) chick peans
- 2 medium carrots, shredded 
- 1 medium onion, chopped 
- 1/4 cup chicken broth. 
- 2 tsp chicken bouillon granules 
- 4 garlic cloves - minced 
- 1 1/2 tsp minced fresh thyme 
- 1/4 tsp fennel seed 
- 1 tsp dried lavender flowers 
- 1/2 lb bacon strips, cooked and crumbled 
- Additional fresh thyme, optional 

## Directions 
1.  In a large skillet, cook sausage over medium heat until no longer pink, drain.  Transfer to a 4-5qt slow cooker.  Add the water, chicken, beans, carrot, onion, broth, bouillon, garlic, thyme, fennel, and if desired, lavender.  Cover and cook on low for 6-8 or until headed through.  Divide among bowls, sprinkle with bacon.  If desired, top with additional fresh thyme.
