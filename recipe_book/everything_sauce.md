# Japanese Everything Sauce

- 4 TBSP soy sauce
- 2 TBSP sugar
- 1 TBSP oyster sauce
- 1/2 TBSP sesame oil
- 2 tsp rice wine vinegar
- 50 ml water
- 1 TBSP sesame seeds
- 1oz green onion, finely chopped
- 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
- dash of MSG (opt)
- 1 g Hondashi powder (opt)
- 1 tsp red pepper flakes or dried red chili (opt)

Mix everything well.

Can use to marinate cooked eggs, to marinate meat, or as a rice
bowl sauce.

For a rice bowl, prepare rice. Marinate pork slices for a couple
minutes, while you saute some cabbage. Saute the pork until done.
Serve over cabbage and pork over rice with additional sauce.
