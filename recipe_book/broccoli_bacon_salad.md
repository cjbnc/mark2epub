# Broccoli and Bacon Salad 
## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup mayo or salad dressing 
- 2 TBSP sugar 
- 2 TBSP white vinegar 
- 1 lb broccoli cut into flowerets (5 cups)
- 1/4 cup chopped red onion 
- 1/4 cup chopped yellow onion 
- 1/4 cup sunflower nuts 
- 6 slices bacon, crisply cooked and crumbled 

## Directions 
1.  Mix mayo, sugar and vinegar in large bowl.  Stir in broccoli and onion.  Toss until coated.  Cover and refrigerate 2 hours to blend flavors.  Sprinkle with nuts and bacon before serving. 

To make it more like Mom's version, use shredded cheddar cheese, pecans and half broccoli/cauliflower.
