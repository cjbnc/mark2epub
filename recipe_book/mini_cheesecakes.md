# Mini Cheesecakes
## Ingredients
- 24 vanilla wafer cookies
- 2 8oz packages cream cheese, softened. 
- 3/4 cup sugar 
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp almond or vanilla extract 
- 1 can pie filling (apple, blueberry, etc)
- 1/2 cup toasted, sliced almonds
## Directions 
1.  Heat oven to 375.  Line 24 regular size muffin cups with paper liners. Place 1 vanilla wafer cookie in bottom of each cup. 
2.  In large bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and almond extract with electric mixer on medium-high speed 1-2 minutes or until mixture is light and fluffy.  Spoon evenly into muffin cups (about 2/3 full). 
3.  Bake 20-25 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.  Remove from muffin cups to cooling rack.  Cool completely, about 30 minutes.  Refrigerate at least 1 hour or up to 24 hours before serving. 
4.  Just before serving, top each cheesecake with generous table pie filling.  Sprinkle with almonds.
