# Crispy Onion Parmesan Chicken 
## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup mayonnaise
- 2 TBSP shredded Parmesan cheese
- 3/4 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 1 TBSP Dijon mustard
- 1/4 cup Panko breadcrumbs
- 1/2 pkg french fried onions, crushed (3oz box)
## Directions 
1.  Preheat oven to 425.  
2.  Stir together mayo and cheese in small bowl. 
3.  Cut each chicken breast in half crosswise and pound to an even thickness.  
4.  Place on foil lined baking sheet. 
5.  Lightly season with salt and pepper;.  Brush with Dijon to coat. 
6.  Spread mayo mixture over chicken. 
7.  Stir together panko and onions.  
8.  Dredge tops of chicken in mixture to coat.  
9.  Return to pan.
10.  Bake 15-20 minutes or until chicken is done.
